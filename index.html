CONDITIONING

EliteFitGym Conditioning is one of our most popular classes suitable for all levels of fitness. This class is fast paced where you will work your way through a series of challenges such as AMRAP’s (As Many Rounds as Possible), RFT’s (Rounds For Time) & EMOM’s (Every Minute on the Minute).

DANCEFIT

Join the EliteFitGym party! Dancefit is one of EliteFitGym’s most popular fitness classes that will feel more like a party than a workout. Each class will go through a series of different dance styles that will improve endurance, fitness and get you super sweaty! A guaranteed mood booster too!

METCON

METCON is short for Metabolic Conditioning. These classes are designed to increase energy usage in short sets of high intensity. You will be working hard throughout the whole class increasing your heart rate, improving your overall fitness level.

PILATES

Pilates conditions your entire body – even your feet! It is a full body and mind workout that focuses on muscle balance, posture alignment, flexibility and core strength. No muscle group is overlooked or overused which will allow you to enjoy daily activities and sports with better performance and less chance of injury.
